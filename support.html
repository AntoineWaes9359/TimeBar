Getting help

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, please contact us by email at the address listed on the App Store product page or in the app’s About section. We’ll do our best to get back to you.

Frequently asked questions

How do I add a time zone?
Open TimeBar from the menubar and choose Settings. Under “Add a location”, enter a name and optional flag/trigram, search for a city or time zone, select it from the list, then click Add.

How do I edit or remove a location?
In Settings, click a location in the list to load it into the form below. Change the details and click Update, or click the − button next to a location to remove it. You can also reorder locations by dragging.

What is the overlap view?
The overlap view shows a 24-hour timeline of your locations. Green indicates when everyone is in a “working” or “awake” window—ideal for scheduling meetings. Click a time slot to copy the time zone and local times to the clipboard for emails or calendar invites.

Which versions of macOS are supported?
TimeBar is designed for recent versions of macOS. Check the App Store listing for the minimum OS version required.
