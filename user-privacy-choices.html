User Privacy Choices

Last updated: February 2026

This page summarizes the privacy choices available to you when using TimeBar and how we handle your information. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Your data stays on your device

TimeBar does not send your data to our servers or to third parties. All information you enter (time zones, locations, preferences) is stored only on your Mac. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including, for example, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California), you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. Because TimeBar does not collect or process your data on our systems:

  • Access / portability: All data the app uses is already on your device. You can view and manage it in the app’s settings.
  • Correction: You can add, edit, or remove locations and preferences at any time in the app.
  • Deletion: To delete all data associated with TimeBar, remove the app from your Mac. You can also remove individual locations from the app’s settings.
  • Opt‑out of “sale” or “sharing”: We do not sell or share your personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of.

No tracking or targeted advertising

TimeBar does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or targeted advertising. The app does not include third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Contact

If you have questions about your privacy choices or want to exercise your rights, please use the contact or support information provided in the App Store listing or in the app.
